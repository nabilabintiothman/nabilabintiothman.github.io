Who I am

My name is Nabila and I am a certified Junior Data Scientist by the Center of Applied Data Science (CADS). A lot of people were quite surprised when I told them that I hold a Bachelor's degree in Biomedical Science from UCL and I have worked for two years as a quality control chemist. But I always felt like this is not something that I want to be doing for my whole life. I was intrigued with this new rising field of Data Science but I didn't know where to start. Then when I got selected for a Data Scientist training program, I became certain that this is my passion. Now after successfully completed the program, I am devoting my time to increase my knowledge and skills in data science.This website is created for me to update my progress in learning Data Science and I had so much fun creating and designing my first website from scratch!
